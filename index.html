About PolyNote

PolyNote helps students and professionals convert audio recordings into accurate transcripts, flashcards, and quizzes using AI. Organize your study materials efficiently and review smarter, not harder.

Key Features

  • On-device audio transcription with AI assistance
  • Generate quizzes, flashcards, and key terms from your recordings
  • Organize content into folders and projects
  • Optional Google Calendar integration for scheduling study sessions
  • Offline core functionality

Data Transparency

PolyNote only sends transcript text to our AI backend (OpenAI) for study material generation. Audio files remain on your device. We do not share your data with third parties without consent.
